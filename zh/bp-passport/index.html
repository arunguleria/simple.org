BP Passport

The world's simplest app to track your blood pressure, blood sugar, and remember to take your medicines.

Free for everyone

The BP Passport app is totally free and will be free forever.

Simple by intention

The BP Passport app is designed to be simple. It is easy to learn and easy to use, even for people who don't have much experience using smartphone applications.

  • Spanish
