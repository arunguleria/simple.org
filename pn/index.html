ਹਾਇਪਰਟੈਂਸ਼ਨ ਦੇ ਰੋਗੀਆਂ ਦਾ ਪਤਾ ਲਗਾਉਣ ਲਈ ਚਿਕਿਤਸਕਾਂ ਲਈ ਤੇਜ਼, ਮੁਫ਼ਤ ਐਪ।

Simple is designed to support large-scale hypertension and NCD management programs. The app is deployed in 580 public health facilities in India and Bangladesh to manage 257,010 patients with hypertension.

Healthcare workers record every patient visit in an app, managers get daily reports to monitor progress, and patients can chart their own BPs.

  1. Simple app for healthcare workers

    An easy-to-use app for healthcare workers to record BPs, blood sugars, and medicines at every patient visit, in less than 20 seconds.

  2. Simple dashboard for monitoring

    A web-based dashboard for health system managers to monitor hypetension control across facilities and regions.

  3. Simple BP Passport app for patients

    A simple iOS and Android app for patients to record BPs/sugars and to remember to take their medications.
